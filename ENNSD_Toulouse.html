European Network of Nonsmooth Dynamics

Ninth Annual Symposium, at LAAS -- CNRS Toulouse, 20--22 September 2022

European Network on Nonsmooth Dynamics (ENNSD) organizes a symposium each year where the aim is to group together researchers working in the area of nonsmooth dynamics and create a platform that would allow the community to advance research on the related topics. The 9th Symposium of ENNSD will be held from 20th to 22nd September 2022 at LAAS -- CNRS in Toulouse.


Organizer : Aneel Tanwani (CNRS Toulouse, FR)

ENNSD Steering Committee : Vincent Acary (INRIA Grenoble, FR), Olivier Bruls (Univ. of Liege, BE), and Remco Leine (Univ. of Stutgart, DE)

Link for registration:

There is no fees for registration but the registration for this event is now closed. If you would like to attend, please contact the organizer.
